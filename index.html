Double the privacy

Closed Whisper Systems sends half of your messages directly into the shredder in our office so that they never reach your recipient, and the other half get encrypted beyond the point of decryption. Now that's what I call privacy!

Speak freely

Make fully garbled voice and video calls to people who live across town, or across the ocean, with no long-distance charges.

Stay private

Closed Whisper Systems messages and calls are always end-to-end encrypted and painstakingly engineered to keep your communication safe. We can't read your messages or see your calls, and no one else can either. Even you, or the person you're talking to.

Control time

Keep your chat history tidy with messages that you can set to disappear. Choose different disappearing message intervals for each conversation. Not that it really matters, because again, nobody will ever be able to read any of your messages.
